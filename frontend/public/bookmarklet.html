Setup · One-time

Review Autofill Bookmarklet

After you save a review in your app, it opens 1001albumsgenerator with your review encoded in the URL. Click the bookmarklet on that tab and it instantly fills the text box for you.

1
Drag the button below to your browser's bookmarks bar. (If the bar isn't visible, press Cmd+Shift+B / Ctrl+Shift+B.)
2
In your app, write your review and click Save Review. A new tab opens on 1001albumsgenerator.
3
Switch to that tab, then click the bookmarklet. Your review text appears in the textarea automatically.
★ Fill Review ← drag this to your bookmarks bar

How it works: When you save, your app encodes your review as Base64 and appends it to the 1001albumsgenerator URL as #review=…. The bookmarklet reads that fragment, decodes it, and fires the React synthetic events needed for the textarea to register the new value. Nothing is sent to any third-party server — it all runs locally in your browser.
